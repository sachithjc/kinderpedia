It’s hard to be a
Preschool Teacher!

Try explaining to little minds, using little words they know, the following :-

  • What languages are, and the meaning of 100s of words
  • What our systems to quantify and calculate are
  • What are colours, shapes, seasons, verbs & words ?
  • And when the medium of instruction is English , a language most kids do not speak at home ??
  • It gets more acute when kids are from different linguistic back grounds as in cities world wide !!

Preschool Teachers must
have prayed really hard for ...

  • A single magic means to get the rapt attention of kids
  • A language without words that all the kids could understand
    (given their very limited verbal vocabulary )
  • A means to teach kids conceptually, quickly & effortlessly

  • Over 35 + apps that make teaching preschool children as easy an 123!
  • Apps that teach children quickly by enablilng them to comprehend concepts behind what they learn in depth! Avoiding learning by rote.
