Kinderpedia is a multimedia-based teaching aid - a learning platform designed to make preschool teaching simpler, more engaging, and truly universal. It helps teachers explain complex early-learning concepts—language, numbers, shapes, colours, seasons, actions, and more—to KG children using limited vocabulary and may come from diverse linguistic backgrounds. Using animations, curated videos, audio, graphics, and bright visuals delivered through intuitive touch-screen apps, Kinderpedia captures children’s attention instantly and teaches them conceptually rather than through rote learning. With over 35 thoughtfully designed apps, it enables children to understand ideas deeply—without relying on words, keyboards, or prior language skills—using a format they are already familiar with from cartoons, tablets, and smartphones. Kinderpedia bridges language gaps and transforms how preschoolers learn, making education natural, effortless, and joyful for both teachers and children.

Why use Kinderpedia?

It’s hard to be a Preschool Teacher! Without creative teaching aids, it can be difficult to explain complex early-learning concepts to children who have not yet grasped how to communicate in any language, let alone, in English - a language that most children do not speak at home!

Concepts like...

  • languages, the meaning of 100s of words,
  • quantifing and calculating,
  • colours, shapes, seasons, verbs & words etc.

...are all likely to be difficult, without the help of teaching aids.
